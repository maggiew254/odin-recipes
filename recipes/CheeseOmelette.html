Cheese Omelette Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 shredded cheddar cheese
  • Chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Combine eggs and milk in bowl; beat with fork or whisk until well mixed. Season with salt and pepper, if desired.
  2. Melt butter in 8-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until sizzling.
  3. Pour egg mixture into hot skillet.
  4. Cook 30 seconds or until bottom is set.
  5. Run heat proof spatula around edge of pan, lifting edges of eggs and allowing uncooked portions to flow underneath.
  6. Continue cooking, lifting eggs as needed, 1-2 minutes or until almost set.
  7. Remove from heat. Sprinkle cheese over half of omelette.
  8. Carefully fold other side of omelette over cheese and slide omelette onto serving plate.
  9. Sprinkle with parsley, if desired.
